### Recipe 2: Aglio e Olio (Spaghetti with Garlic and Olive Oil)
- **Calories per serving:** Approximately 400
- **Preparation Time:** 10 minutes
- **Cooking Time:** 8 minutes
- **Allergens:** Gluten (in pasta)
- **Steps:**
  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil and cook the spaghetti until al dente. Reserve 1 cup of pasta water before draining the spaghetti.
  2. In a large skillet, heat a generous amount of olive oil over medium-low heat.
  3. Add thinly sliced garlic and cook, stirring occasionally, until the garlic is lightly golden (about 4-5 minutes).
  4. Add red pepper flakes (if using) and cook for another minute.
  5. Add the reserved pasta water to the skillet in small increments to create a light sauce.
  6. Combine the cooked spaghetti with the garlic and oil mixture, tossing everything together.

### Recipe 3: Pasta with Cheese
- **Calories per serving:** Approximately 450
- **Preparation Time:** 5 minutes

  3. Add grated cheese (such as parmesan or cheddar) and stir until melted and smooth.
